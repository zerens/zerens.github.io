ABOUT ME

Currently I am a PostDoc Researcher, working with Prof. Yazhou Yao @ School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2021 from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, supervised by Prof. Zhenmin Tang. I obtained my Master degree in 2016 from Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, supervised by Prof. Mel Siegel. I obtained my Bachelor degree in 2014 from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology.

Research Interests: Learning from Noise, Fine-grained Visual Categorization, Computer Vision, Deep Learning
