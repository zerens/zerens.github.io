ABOUT ME

Currently I am a Ph.D. candidate, working with Prof. Zhenmin Tang and Prof. Yazhou Yao @ School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. I obtained my master degree in 2016 from Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University, supervised by Mel Siegel. I obtained my Bachelor degree in 2014 from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology.

Research Interests: Learning from Noise, Fine-grained Visual Categorization, Computer Vision, Deep Learning
